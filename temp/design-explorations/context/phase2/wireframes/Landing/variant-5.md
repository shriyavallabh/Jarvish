- **Soft shadows** add depth without intimidation
- **Rounded corners (12-16px)** everywhere for approachability
- **Hand-drawn illustrations** humanize the technology

### Trust Building Elements
- Conversational microcopy reduces intimidation
- Real person testimonials with photos
- Process visualization makes complex simple
- Friendly emojis used sparingly but effectively

### Typography Hierarchy
- **H1**: Fraunces 44px - Warm, friendly serif
- **H2**: Fraunces 28px - Section headers
- **Body**: Open Sans 16px/26px - Easy reading
- **Microcopy**: Open Sans 14px - Helpful hints
