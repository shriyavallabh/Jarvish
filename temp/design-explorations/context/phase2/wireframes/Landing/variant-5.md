## Design Rationale

### Visual Identity
- **Off-white background (#FAF9F7)** creates warmth without harshness
- **Soft shadows** add depth without intimidation
- **Rounded corners (12-16px)** everywhere for approachability
- **Hand-drawn illustrations** humanize the technology

### Trust Building Elements
- Conversational microcopy reduces intimidation
- Real person testimonials with photos
- Process visualization makes complex simple
- Friendly emojis used sparingly but effectively

### Typography Hierarchy
- **H1**: Fraunces 44px - Warm, friendly serif
- **H2**: Fraunces 28px - Section headers
- **Body**: Open Sans 16px/26px - Easy reading
- **Microcopy**: Open Sans 14px - Helpful hints

## Accessibility Considerations

### WCAG AA Compliance
- **Brown on white**: 9.2:1 contrast ratio ✓
- **Body text**: 12.6:1 contrast ratio ✓
- **Green on white**: 4.8:1 contrast ratio ✓
- **Touch targets**: 48px minimum everywhere

### Friendly Accessibility
- **Clear language**: No jargon, simple explanations
- **Visual hierarchy**: Clear sections and flow
- **Helper text**: Inline guidance for forms
- **Error messages**: Friendly, helpful tone

### Screen Reader Support
- Descriptive alt text for illustrations
- Proper heading structure
- Form labels and descriptions
- Skip navigation links

### Cognitive Accessibility
- Simple, scannable layouts
- Consistent patterns throughout
- Clear calls-to-action
- Progress indicators for multi-step processes

### Performance
- Optimized illustrations (SVG)
- Lazy loading for below-fold content
- Smooth but not distracting animations
- Fast load times for trust building
